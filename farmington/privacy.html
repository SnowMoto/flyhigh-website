Privacy Policy

This statement applies to FLY HIGH ADVENTURE PARKS.

As operator of the www.flyhighfun.com website, Fly High Adventure Parks (“Fly High”) will, from time to time, collect personal information from you.

This privacy policy sets out how Fly High uses and protects any information that you give when you use this website.

Fly High is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide personal information, we assure you that this information will be used in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the National Privacy Principals (“NPPs”).

Fly High may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. This policy is effective from January 1, 2018.

As you browse our site, advertising cookies may be placed on your computer so that we can understand what you are interested in. This enables re-targeting ads based on your interaction with Fly High.

We collect personal information when you:

  • Create a Roller account via the sign-up page or booking process
  • Confirm and pay for products/services
  • Enter a competition
  • Complete a survey
  • Provide feedback

Types of personal information collected:

  • Name and contact details
  • Email address
  • Transaction details
  • Any stated preferences

We use personal information to provide requested services, process bookings, and contact you about changes or cancellations. We may also use it for marketing and research unless you opt out.

We may disclose your personal information to venues, promoters, or as required by law.

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. Contact us at admin@flyhightrampolinepark.com.

We take steps to safeguard your personal information through physical, electronic, and managerial measures.

  • We do not adopt/share unique identifiers unless necessary
  • We do not transfer your data internationally unless privacy standards match
  • We do not store card details on our website

We are not responsible for unauthorized access to personal information or the privacy practices of third-party websites linked from ours.
